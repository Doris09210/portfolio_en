Personal Story

Hi, I'm Du Yao - currently still exploring in the Design Science program at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Southeast University. My research interests are simple: making AI more usable through cognitive science, making complex information simpler through design.

In my studies at Southeast University's School of Mechanical Engineering Design Science program, I focus on applying cognitive psychology theories to practical interface design. Through Unity interactive prototypes, combined with eye-tracking and behavioral data analysis, I attempt to understand users' cognitive processes when interacting with AI systems, and optimize interface design based on these insights.

I believe that future AI systems must not only be intelligent, but also "thoughtful." By deeply understanding human cognitive characteristics and interaction habits, we can design more human-centered and efficient AI interfaces, making technology truly serve humanity.
